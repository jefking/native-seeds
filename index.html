Native Seeds

Welcome to Native Seeds, where our roots run deep in the soil and culture of British Columbia. As an Indigenous-run company, we are proud to offer specialized seed mixes tailored specifically for construction and restoration projects in our region. Our seeds are more than just ground cover—they're a commitment to preserving the native flora and minimizing soil erosion in British Columbia.
Our unique selection of native plants not only stabilizes the land but also supports the biodiversity that makes our area unique. Choose Native Seeds—where every seed planted helps protect our heritage and the natural beauty of British Columbia.
